DonateNow

Empowering Blood Banks for Consistent Blood Flow

Welcome to DonateNow, where we strive to create a sustainable solution for blood banks in India by facilitating easy communication with potential donors.

Currently the website and project is under construction and in the making. It should be up and running with one partnenered blood bank by January.

About Us

DonateNow is a project initiated by a group of Computer Science majors at Manipal Institute of Technology. Our mission is to address the critical need for a consistent blood supply in India.

Vision and Mission

Vision: A future where every blood bank in India has access to a steady and reliable supply of blood to save lives.

Mission: To connect blood banks with potential donors through an efficient and user-friendly platform, ensuring a timely and continuous flow of blood donations.

Idea Statement

Recognizing the expiration of blood within 40 days and inspired by the success of blood donation camps, DonateNow focuses on creating an online platform that allows blood banks to reach out to specific blood groups for timely and consistent donations throughout the year.
